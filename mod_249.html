Mod 249

Adds custom tiles to map mods. To server owners, feel free to add our mods to your modpacks. We grant permission to all. For Modders: Editor tilesheets are found within the google drive or directly through the mod in the resource folder, place them in your Tiles\2x\ folder with the others. Your mod.info needs to have this line: require=DylansTiles If you use the tiles in your map make sure on the mod steam page you make it a requirement to use this mod.
