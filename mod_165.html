Hunter's Base In the deep forests of Riverside lived a seasoned old hunter. When the apocalypse had come he acted quickly and made sure his home would hold in this twisted world. Since there it is, his lake for fish. High fences and some plot to farm for the long haul. What could go wrong... Features: + A cosy livingroom with a fireplace. + A kitchen with a small pantry with shelves and freezers. + An armory with enough storage. + A master bedroom with tv. + A library. + An attic currently used as a clothing storage. + A garage with many shelves. + Two plots of land for farming. + Tall fences that cover entire base. + A lake for fishing. Located south of Riverside. It occupies the cells 20x19. Does not conflict with SecretZ Checkpoint 6.
