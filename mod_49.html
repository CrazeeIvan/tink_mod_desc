Mod 49

Reduces weights with the intention of making armor more focused on zomboid protection at the cost of bullet defense. The original armor pieces were thick enough to deflect bullets, but zomboids were able to bite through them? This also prevents all armor pieces from getting holes. This tweak was built for my personal PvE server. Planned: Minor forearm armor from shoulder and handplates, possibly a new armor piece.
