Mod 219

Under the cover of night, the USS Volfram Amorosa docked along the Ohio River. It's mission: infiltrate, investigate, and unravel the chaos that besieges the land. Please Read- Cell 41,21. No known conflicts. But I will add any to the description if they are found. Situated between West Point and on the road up to Louisville. Includes an in game map. Includes Foraging. Includes a spawn point. Works in multiplayer. The Frigate is worth visiting as it contains weapons and military items, though be careful... the tight corridors below the decks make it a hazardous place to visit. Recommended mod- Choose to spawn on the Frigate and then the Insurgent Profession for a great play through starting point.
