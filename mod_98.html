Mod 98

Complete overhaul to item categories and various name tweaks. Compatible with B41, B40, and Multiplayer(B40+B41)! This mod can be safely added or removed from any Saved game. WTF is "Item"? Sounds too vague... Enhanced categories for all items including many modded ones! Get meaningful item categories and sort your inventory better! Support for over 200 other workshop mods is included!
