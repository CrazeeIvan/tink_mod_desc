About the Map: A generally quiet river town, Rabbit Hash is known for its iconic General Store. Here, you will find all manner of goodies to help you out on your journey along the rural route to Louisville. Rabbit Hash is a real-world location in KY, although it has been displaced a bit geographically and now finds itself a fair ways down the river from its former home.
