Mod 72

This mod allows to repair Deep Cycle batteries for ISA v41 To repair it you will need small batteris, bleach, water and some other items and tools. Restored condition depends on Electricity perk and Luck/Unluck trait. Also you can get a burn on a random body part during battery repair. (Work with chemical can cause chemical burn injury if you have a bad luck). Chance depends on Electricity perk and Luck/Unluck trait and Drunk moodle.
